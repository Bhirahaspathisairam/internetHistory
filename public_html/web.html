Beginning of Web

In 1980 it was difficult to search the internet without the wizard and by the time internet was released to more public. At that time Tim Berners-Lee, a British scientist, invented the World Wide Web (WWW) in 1989, while working at CERN. The basic idea of the WWW was to merge the evolving technologies of computers, data networks and hypertext into a powerful and easy to use global information system.
